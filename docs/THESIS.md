# Thesis

## One-Sentence Thesis

Build an autonomous R&D engine for machine learning that turns fresh literature and code into ranked hypotheses, reproducible experiments, and evidence-backed conclusions.

## Why This, Not Another Deep-Research App

The browsing-and-synthesis layer is already crowded. The harder and more durable problem is trustworthy discovery:

- structured prior-art understanding
- contradiction detection
- hypothesis generation
- experiment planning and execution
- artifact-backed reporting

## Wedge

Start with ML/LLMs.

Why:

- public papers and repos
- benchmark-driven feedback loops
- executable experiments
- faster iteration than most non-software sciences
- natural adjacency to PwnKit and model/system research

## Company vs Product

- Company vision: autonomous empirical discovery
- Product v1: autonomous ML research engine
- Expansion path: adjacent empirical domains after the loop is reliable
